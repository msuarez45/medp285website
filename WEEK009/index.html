About

Myself

I am a Hispanic Graphic Designer, born and raised in the Bronx. I come from a family of artists. My mother practiced fashion design and made a good portion of our clothes when my siblings and I were younger. My older sister was a fashion designer and when to Fashion Institute of Design. I am an artist because of their influence. My work is simple but clean and effective. It is to the point without being dull.

Simple and quikc train sketches. I wanted to add variety in my portfolio so I added these timed sketches. You have no idea when the person will move or if they arrived at their stop, so you have to get as much detail as possible drawing on the train.

Owl Project

Scratch Board

This owl was done using a scratch board. It is a black board with a white backpiece. I had o scratch off the black portion of the scratch board to reveal the white underneath. The piece must be planned and must be worked on with light hands. I f you scratch too much black off, you're stuck with the mark you created.
